Interests

Luna

One of the current obsessions in my life is my puppy Luna. Luna is yellow lab puppy that is constantly full of energy and is always extremely happy. She always has to be around me, if she isn't able to have some part of her body touching me. She loves attention and going for walks, especially if there is a bridge during our walk. She is kennel trained, but no longer knows it as her kennel. It has been named Azkabahn and she only knows it as this now, even if she isn't sure where the reference comes from. When she isn't running around playing and requesting attention, she loves to cuddle with me while watching T.V.
